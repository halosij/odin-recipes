Sea Breeze

Description:

A perfeclty balanced drink for late night summer days.

Ingredients:

  • 35ml vodka
  • 50ml grapefruit juice
  • 25ml cranberry juice
  • Ice
  • Lime

How to make:

  1. Put some ice in your glass
  2. Add the vodka
  3. Add grapefruit juice
  4. Stirr a bit and add cranberry juice
  5. Press out some lime juice and add it as decoration
  6. Enjoy!!
