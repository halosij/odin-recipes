Feta Pasta

Return to main page

Description:

A wonderful oven pasta with feta cheese and tomatos.

Ingredients:

  • Feta cheese
  • Cherry or normal tomatos
  • Olive oil
  • Pasta of choice
  • Seasonings such as:
    • Basilicum
    • Oregano
    • Garlic powder
    • Paprika powder
    • Salt

How to make:

  1. Put the feta in the middle of a baking dish which is safe for oven
  2. Surround the cheese with cutted tomatos
  3. Put enough seasoning in and after that a bit olive oil everywhere
  4. Put the baking dish now in a preheated oven on 170Â°C circulating air for 25-30min
  5. Now cook your pasta of choice
  6. After you pull out your dish out of the oven stirr it so as a result u get a nice sauce
  7. Add the pasta and stirr it again
  8. Enjoy!!
