Red Vodka Bowle

Description:

A wonderful looking red vodka bowl. Perfectly suited for grill parties in the summer!

Ingredients:

  • Ice (normal or crushed)
  • Red frozen fruits such as:
    • Pomegranate seeds
    • Strawberries
    • blackberries
    • currant
  • 1l Cherry lemonade
  • 1l Pomegranate lemonade
  • 0.75l Bitter lemon
  • 0.7 - 1l Vodka
  • Fresh mint
  • Lime
  • A big bowl

How to make:

  1. Put the Ice and the fruits into the bowl
  2. Add Vodka
  3. Add all lemonades to the bowl
  4. Stirr the bowle
  5. Cut the lime into square pieces
  6. Press out a bit lime juice into the bowle and add the pieces
  7. Add some mint
  8. Enjoy!!
